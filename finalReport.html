Health Data Visualization

Final Project Report

Project Name : Visualising Health Data - An initiative towards creating public awareness about health

Project Contributors : Chandrasekhar Ananthabhotla (RA2011003010591)

Project Concept : To use the "National Family Health Survey - 5 (2019-2021)" data and depict demographic visualizations out of it.

Visualization Goals : I have made interesting visualizations using various attributes from the dataset which will help people in gaining health knowledge easily.

Intended Audience : General Public, Doctors, Health Policymakers, Pharmaceutical Industries, Product-Based Tech Firms.

Testimonials : I have tried my level best to collect testemonials from the most relevant stakeholders for this project. I would like to appreciate the following people for coming forward to speak a couple of words about this project.

Data Source : https://data.gov.in

Open Government Data Platform India or data.gov.in is a platform for supporting Open data initiative of Government of India. This portal is a single-point access to datasets, documents, services, tools and applications published by ministries, departments and organisations of the Government of India.
